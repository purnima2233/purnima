VUDA Park or VMRDA Park is an urban park located near RK Beach in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India. It is operated by the Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region Development Authority (VMRDA). It contains gardens, a view of the Bay of Bengal, rides on horses and camels, and a skating rink. The VUDA Park was established to provide the growing city of Visakhapatnam with an urban recreational space. Since its opening to the public, it has drawn in visitors with its wide array of attractions, including well-manicured landscapes, a musical fountain, a skating rink, and a boating facility.
